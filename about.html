About Bubu & Dudu Flappy

Bubu & Dudu Flappy started as a small experiment to build a warm, replayable flying bear game that anyone could enjoy in seconds. The idea was simple: take the familiar tension of a flappy style arcade game and combine it with friendly characters, cheerful visuals, and quick browser access. Bubu leads with bold energy, while Dudu adds a loyal companion vibe that makes each run feel more alive.

The project is an indie HTML5 browser game focused on lightweight performance. We want players to open a page and play instantly without app stores, logins, or heavy downloads. That makes it easy for casual players on desktop and mobile to jump in, chase a score, and come back later for one more run.

We also care about making the website feel complete, not just functional. That means clear game guides, character pages, policy pages, and support contact details. A structured site helps new players understand what they are playing and helps returning players trust that the project is actively maintained.

Our long-term goal is to grow this into a small, family-friendly portal of cute casual game ideas. Future plans include memory, puzzle, and timing-based games set in the same Bubu and Dudu world. We are continuously refining controls, visual polish, and content pages so the website becomes a complete game destination rather than a single standalone title.
