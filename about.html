About Bubu & Dudu Flappy

Bubu & Dudu Flappy began as a small idea: build a playful flying bear game that feels warm, simple, and easy to pick up in seconds. Bubu is the bold lead flyer, while Dudu follows with a cute companion energy that makes each run feel more alive. Together, they turn a classic flappy style arcade game into a friendly world that players of any age can enjoy.

This project is an indie HTML5 browser game built for fast performance and instant access. You can play in modern browsers on desktop and mobile without downloading large files or installing extra software. The design focuses on responsive controls, smooth gameplay, and clear visual feedback so players can quickly improve timing and rhythm.

Our purpose is to create fun, simple casual games that are light, colorful, and replayable. Bubu & Dudu Flappy is the first step toward a growing mini game portal featuring more bite-size browser titles with the same cute style. Future releases will include puzzle, memory, and arcade ideas built around Bubu and Dudu as recurring characters.

If you enjoy the online flappy game experience, we appreciate your support and feedback. Every suggestion helps improve gameplay balance, accessibility, and future game concepts.
