Bubu & Dudu Flappy

Welcome to the Bubu and Dudu game, a cute casual game where two lovable bears fly through a honey-themed sky. This online flappy game is built for fun sessions and score chasing.

Game Description

Bubu & Dudu Flappy is a flappy style arcade game where Bubu leads and Dudu follows through moving obstacles and tight gaps. Gravity pulls them down, so timing each flap is the key to survival. The objective is simple: fly farther, pass more obstacles, and beat your best score.

As an HTML5 browser game, it runs directly in modern browsers with no installation. It is tuned for desktop and mobile play, making it a lightweight flying bear game you can enjoy anywhere.

How to Play

Start the run and guide Bubu through each obstacle gap. Every clean pass increases your score. If you hit an obstacle or the ground, the round ends. Stay calm, flap in short bursts, and keep your height steady before each gap.

Controls

Desktop

  • Spacebar - Flap
  • Mouse Click - Flap

Mobile / Tablet

  • Tap Screen - Flap

FAQ

What is Bubu & Dudu Flappy?

It is a cute casual game built as an HTML5 browser game. The main goal is to fly through obstacles and chase a higher score each run.

How do I control the bears?

On desktop, press spacebar or click the mouse to flap. On mobile, tap anywhere on the game canvas. Good timing matters more than fast tapping.

Can I play on mobile devices?

Yes. This online flappy game is responsive and optimized for mobile browsers and tablets. You can play directly without downloading an app.

Is the game free to play?

Yes, the Bubu and Dudu game is free to play in your browser. You can replay unlimited rounds and keep improving your high score.

Who created the game?

Bubu & Dudu Flappy is an indie web project focused on fun, fast, accessible arcade gameplay. The goal is to expand this into a small browser game portal over time.
